How It Works

The AI question generator creates questions based on the course name and difficulty level you select. It uses templates and patterns to generate questions that are relevant to your course.

Question Types:
  • Multiple Choice: Questions with one correct answer out of four options.
  • Checkbox: Questions with multiple correct answers that students must select.
  • Short Answer: Questions that require students to enter text answers, matched against keywords.
Difficulty Levels:
  • Easy: Basic concept questions, suitable for beginners.
  • Medium: Intermediate questions that test understanding of course concepts.
  • Hard: Advanced questions that test deeper knowledge and application.
